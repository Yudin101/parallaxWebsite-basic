JDM Cars

JDM (Japanese Domestic Market) cars are vehicles that are designed and manufactured for sale in Japan. These cars are known for their high performance, reliability, and unique styling, and have gained a dedicated following among car enthusiasts around the world.

Toyota Supra

The Toyota Supra is a legendary sports car that was first introduced in 1978. The fourth-generation model, produced from 1993 to 2002, is particularly famous for its powerful engines, exceptional handling, and distinctive, aerodynamic design. Today, the Supra remains highly sought-after among car enthusiasts, and a new fifth-generation model has recently been introduced to critical acclaim.

Nissan Skyline

Nissan Skyline

The Nissan Skyline is a line of compact cars that has gained a reputation as a high-performance sports car. The Skyline is particularly famous for its third-generation models, including the GT-R and GT-R Nismo, which were designed for racing and featured advanced technologies such as front and rear spoilers and a limited-slip differential. The Skyline remains a favorite among car enthusiasts around the world.

Toyota AE86

Toyota AE86

The Toyota AE86 is a small, lightweight sports car that was first introduced in 1983. The car is particularly famous for its rear-wheel drive layout, responsive handling, and high-revving engine, which produced up to 130 horsepower. The AE86 has become a favorite among car enthusiasts and is often used in drifting competitions.

Mazda RX-7

Mazda RX-7

The Mazda RX-7 is a sports car that was produced by Mazda from 1978 to 2002. The car is particularly famous for its unique rotary engine, which produced high levels of power and was known for its smooth, rev-happy character. The RX-7's sleek styling and responsive handling have made it a favorite among car enthusiasts around the world.

Subaru WRX

Subaru WRX

Honda NSX

Honda NSX

The Honda NSX, also known as the Acura NSX in North America, is a mid-engine sports car produced by Honda. It was first introduced in 1990 and was the first mass-produced car to feature an all-aluminum body. The NSX is known for its exceptional handling and performance, and was designed in collaboration with the legendary Formula One driver Ayrton Senna.
